Carvey!

The Carvey is a desktop CNC machine. It works kind of like the vinyl cutter, but with a drill bit instead of a blade, and it is capable of cutting (or carving) through a much wider variety of materials. The Carvey is capable of cutting through wood, plastic, and even metal. Much like the vinyl cutter, the Carvey just interprets a series of movements - called the "toolpath" - from a controlling computer. Unlike the vinyl cutter, the Carvey operates in three dimensions - meaning you control how deeply you carve into the material you're working with.

Carvey Project #1

Design a coaster! Use Mr. Stone's Coaster as a template (go to File -> Make a Copy). Make sure to rename it by clicking the name at the top.

The coaster is already sized at 3.5 x 3.5 inches with a through-cut all the way around. Add whatever elements you'd like, but remember that you need to keep them large enough for the blade to actually carve out - any sections the blade will skip will be highlighted in red on the preview screen.
